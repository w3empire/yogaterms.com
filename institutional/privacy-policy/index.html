Privacy Policy for YogaTerms

Last Updated: Oct. 22, 2023.

1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at YogaTerms. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Automatically Collected Data

Whenever you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Pages viewed
  • Time spent on pages
  • Other standard server log information

2.2. Data from Third Parties

We may obtain information from third-party services like Google Analytics to understand our website’s usage and improve the user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Analyze and monitor website usage to improve our site’s functionality and user experience.
  • Display relevant advertisements using Google AdSense.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to store and collect information about your preferences and navigation to and from our site. This includes using third-party services, such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense, which may place cookies or web beacons on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services, including:

5.1. Google AdSense

This service is used to display advertisements for our users. Google AdSense may use information (not including your name, address, or email address) about your visits to this and other websites to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

5.2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to measure and understand our site’s performance and user behavior. Google Analytics may capture your IP address, but no other personal information is captured by Google Analytics.

6. Your Choices

You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of our site may not function properly without cookies.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this policy.
